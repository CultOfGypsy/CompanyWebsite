About Me


Mark Carter has been a working musician in Southern California since 1973. His range of performances extend from solo guitar settings, to cruise ships and large outdoor concerts. He has served the San Gabriel Valley, educating aspiring musicians for over 3 decades.



Learn in any of these styles

Classic Rock of the 70's and 80's

Today's Hit Music ala Jason Mraz/Bruno Mars/Taylor Swift/etc.

Country Music (from Johnny Cash to Brad Paisley)

Swing, Traditional, Jazz and Modern ("Smooth") Jazz


Also: I can show you other styles like funk, r&b, disco, reggae, bebop, classical, heavy metal, modern Christian rock, bluegrass, and more!

Why sign up?

What this method offers


Basic Song Structure

I will go over chord interaction and explain the theory behind the music. From Joni Mitchell to Adele, or from the Beatles to Muse, they all have threads that keep us listening.

Lead Guitar Fundamentals

Want to fly like an Eddie, bend like B.B., or soar like Gilmour? Scales, modes, arpeggios, and various ways to approach taking a solo are all included.

Examples

All lessons will include downloadable TAB and sheet music in PDF format.



All Levels

Every student is given equal attention. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced player, there's a lesson for everyone!

Save Money

Save your gas money for new strings. Watch the video over if you missed something. Take advantage of the larger membership packages.

Fun!

Enthusiasm is my specialty! Come prepared to learn while enjoying your session(s) & feel free to ask questions
